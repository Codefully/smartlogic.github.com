Brookes Publishing works with SmartLogic to add features to and maintain their Ages and Stages Questionnaires, two developmental screeners designed to help professionals identify and address developmental delays in children 2 to 66 months of age.

DinnerTime

DinnerTime is a web application providing personalized meal plans based upon the user’s time, budget, health conditions, tastes, and store sales. We’ve worked with DinnerTime from the beginning of their product development process. We help DinnerTime manage their product roadmap and develop their consumer-facing application, which is built in Backbone.js.

Dr. Strange App

Our client Dr. Strange App created "Fake Conversation," an app with over 3 million downloads that lets people stage fake conversations for fun. We worked with Dr. Strange App to build a new application that would work well with the latest version of iOS. We migrated their data to the new app, and integrated it with Flurry, an IOS analytics and advertising SDK.
