b2Bmore - Connecting Companies in Maryland

Join SmartLogic, the Economic Alliance of Greater Baltimore, TEDCO and the Emerging Technology Centers at b2Bmore.

b2Bmore is a business expo designed to connect innovative service providers with larger companies looking to meet and work with partners to build cutting-edge products/technologies.

An exact date has not been set but expect a mid-week morning event this summer, more details will follow.
