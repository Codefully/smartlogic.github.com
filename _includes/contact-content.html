Location and Parking

We're located at the Broom Factory in Canton. Our address is 3500 Boston St, Ste 323, Baltimore, MD 21224 (Google Maps link).

To visit, dial 323 on the call pad located at the front or side entrance. We're located on the third floor, to the right as you exit the staircase.

Important parking info:If you will be visiting for under two hours, park out front of the office. Otherwise park on the street or behind the building. Please obey all parking signs - they do tow and ticket!
