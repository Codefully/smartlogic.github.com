Director of Marketing & Community Engagement

Abstract / Overview

We are seeking someone to help guide our company through its next phase of growth. We need help with (1) generating sales opportunities (i.e. managing the lead generation process); and (2) better engaging with and serving the community (i.e. managing our community engagement).

While nothing is ever perfect, we are confident in our processes for closing sales opportunities and fulfilling the work we sell (i.e. managing the development process).

We do great work—for our clients, employees, and the community—but we need your help to do a better job of letting the community know.

If interested in the position, email your resume to Yair at yair@smartlogic.io.

Goals

  1. Build and manage lead generation processes that increase the quantity and quality of sales opportunities we generate
  2. Increase SmartLogic’s visibility in the community
  3. Help us be the best corporate citizen we can be
  4. Assist our Director of Client Services (Brian) in closing sales opportunities

Our Expectations

First and foremost: we expect you to help us shape this position so that it best reflects the needs of the company, the team, and yourself. Therefore we expect that our expectations today will change significantly, and quickly.

Tactical Expectations of Your Job Duties

  1. Develop and manage marketing strategies and programs (webinars, lead capture materials, lead nurturing campaigns).
  2. Organize events that support our marketing strategy.
  3. Represent SmartLogic at events where it makes sense for us to be represented.
  4. Manage our sponsorship budget, and represent us at events we sponsor.
  5. Determine annual and quarterly marketing performance and budget by forecasting and developing marketing KPIs.
  6. Evaluate customer research, market conditions and competitor data.
  7. Develop proper company messaging and materials.
  8. Generate collateral, including pamphlets, flyers, PDF’s, landing pages, etc.
  9. Manage our email newsletter.
  10. Manage our social media properties.
  11. Manage the content on our website, including all landing pages.
  12. Manage SEM in concert with content on the website, SEO, social media.
  13. Gracefully hand leads over to Brian, our Director of Client Services.

Expectations of You and Your Background / Experience

Things we expect of the person that will take this job (and the criteria by which we’ll evaluate job candidates):

  1. Demonstrated marketing experience: we are not seeking a “junior” or “intro” level employee for this position. We do not have staff with extensive marketing experience and are not in a position to train someone. Therefore we need you to have demonstrated experience in marketing strategy and execution. While we do not expect you to know everything and have every skill right now, we do expect you to be able to quickly learn on the job.
  2. Organized: we value organization and are very organized. The person that will work well in this role will be highly organized.
  3. Strong interpersonal communication: we need someone that is a team player and that doesn’t get on the defensive or take things personally. Our objective as a team is simply to do the best possible work.
  4. Effective communicator: good organization and good communication means the information your other team members need will always be at their fingertips (through various docs, spreadsheets, third party websites, etc.).
  5. Self-starter, entrepreneurial: we don’t micromanage. We work as a team to brainstorm ideas—but you need to bring your own ideas to the table too. We’re a very creative group and do things our own way—you’ll be expected to bring new ideas, processes, and approaches to the table for execution.
  6. Creative: new ideas are welcome. We are small and nimble and fast to do things—experimentation is encouraged!
  7. Work hard, play hard: our team has a very good work ethic. But everyone on the team also has a strong personality, and strong personal life.
  8. Be technical: while we don’t expect you to be a front or back-end developer we do expect you to have solid footing in the world of web and mobile application development. More importantly, you should have a genuine curiosity for software development, design, and programming; we can guarantee that you will learn a tremendous amount by working with our team.

About SmartLogic

We are a bootstrapped custom software development company based out of Baltimore. Our clients range from funded startups to Fortune 10 companies.

We have a strong team of experienced developers and designers. We do great work and our clients love us. We have an efficient and effective but ever-evolving process for software development; our work is never done but we continue to get better at what we do.

Our focus on effective communication—both verbal and through various other channels (github, campfire, google docs, etc.)—helps us consistently meet and exceed client expectations.
